What is Gone Goat?

Gone Goat is a card game about chaos, mischief, and goats that never stay where you put them. It's easy to teach, fast to play, and gets louder every time someone new pulls up a chair.

Each round builds tension as players read each other's moves, time their plays, and try not to be the one left holding everything when the goats finally bolt. Expect last-second saves, honest betrayals, and immediate demands for a rematch.

Everything fits in a standard poker-size tuck box â€” pocket-sized, shelf-worthy, and ready to hit the table in under five minutes.
